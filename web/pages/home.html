Qudwa is a global forum with teachers at its heart. Organised under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi, the event will take place at Emirates Palace on October 7 and 8, 2017.

Qudwa aims to achieve greater recognition for the teaching profession. It will bring together more than 800 local and international participants, who will share insights and best practices to empower teachers and inspire the minds of tomorrow. Participation is by invitation only.

The forum, designed by teachers, for teachers, will explore the topic of Teaching for Tomorrow through interactive sessions, discussions, and workshops with experts, leaders, and peers from around the world.

The event will be held in partnership with global organisations such as the OECD, the Varkey Foundation, and Teach for All, as well as with local partners like the UAE Ministry of Education, the Abu Dhabi Education Council, ACTVET, and the Knowledge and Human Development Authority.

The forum will be attended by teachers, representatives from not-for-profit organisations, and industry experts.
